IXUS220HS / ELPH300HS Notes

To enter CHDK <Alt> mode use the Video button. Maybe this will be changed later to Playback button, but in this first version you have to use Video button.

Short Press of Video button will toggle <ALT> mode.
Long Press of Video button will execute original Canon functionality.

Support for dual partition is not available.

For more information please refer to IXUS200HS/ELPH300HS Porting Thread:
http://chdk.setepontos.com/index.php?topic=6341
